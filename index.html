Welcome to Deciy Expeditions

Deciy Expeditions is a tours and travel company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We understand that travel can be a once-in-a-lifetime experience, and we work hard to make sure that our clients get the most out of their trip. We offer a wide range of travel services, including hotel bookings, airport transfers, tour packages, and more, all designed to provide our clients with the ultimate travel experience. But we are more than just a travel company. We pride ourselves on being uniquely experienced in introducing travelers to the various aspects of Kenya's culture, history, and customs. Our team of travel experts has a deep understanding of the local community, and we are well-equipped to share this knowledge with our clients. We believe that by doing so, we can provide our clients with a more meaningful travel experience that goes beyond just seeing the sights.
