4 DAYS, 3 NIGHTS LAKE NAKURU MASAI MARA HOT AIR BALLOON SAFARI

From USD 1,774

DESCRIPTION

Experience the magic of Kenya’s top safari destinations on this 4-day, 3-night adventure. Explore the flamingo-lined shores of Lake Nakuru and spot rhinos in the acacia woodlands. Continue to the world-famous Masai Mara, home to the Big Five and the great migration. Enjoy thrilling game drives and a once-in-a-lifetime hot air balloon safari over the Mara plains. Accommodation is in carefully selected lodges that combine comfort with breathtaking views.

HIGHLIGHTS

Scenic drive through the Great Rift Valley
Flamingos and rhinos at Lake Nakuru
Game drives in Masai Mara Game Reserve
Hot air balloon safari over the Mara
Big Five sightings
Cultural visit to a Maasai village (optional)
Accommodation in luxury safari lodges

ITINERARY

1

NAIROBI – LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK

Pick-up from your Nairobi hotel or airport and depart for Lake Nakuru National Park. Enjoy a leisurely drive through the Great Rift Valley with scenic stops en route. Arrive at the lodge in time for lunch. Later, proceed for an afternoon game drive to spot endangered rhinos, Rothschild giraffes, buffalo, and vibrant birdlife including flamingos along the lake shore.

Lake Nakuru Sopa Lodge or similar

Lunch and Dinner

2

LAKE NAKURU – MASAI MARA GAME RESERVE

After breakfast, depart Lake Nakuru and drive through the scenic Mau Escarpment towards Masai Mara. Arrive in time for lunch at the lodge followed by an afternoon game drive in the vast savannah. Look out for lions, elephants, cheetahs, and large herds of wildebeest and zebras roaming the plains.

Mara Serena Safari Lodge or similar

Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

3

MASAI MARA – BALLOON SAFARI & GAME VIEWING

Early morning transfer for your hot air balloon safari over the Mara at sunrise — a breathtaking experience followed by a bush breakfast. Return to the lodge for mid-morning relaxation. Later, enjoy a full-day game drive with picnic lunch in the reserve, visiting the Mara River and other rich wildlife habitats.

Mara Serena Safari Lodge or similar

Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

4

MASAI MARA – NAIROBI

After breakfast, check out and enjoy a final scenic drive back to Nairobi. Arrive in the afternoon and proceed for lunch at the Carnivore Restaurant. Later, transfer to your hotel or the airport depending on your departure schedule.

Breakfast and Lunch
