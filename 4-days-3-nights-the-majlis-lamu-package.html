4 DAYS, 3 NIGHTS THE MAJLIS LAMU PACKAGE

From KES 84,900

DESCRIPTION

Explore the laid-back charm of Lamu Island with this 4-day beach escape to The Majlis. Accessible via SGR and domestic flight, this experience blends culture, relaxation, and coastal luxury. From dhow cruises and historic town tours to palm-lined beaches and Swahili-inspired cuisine, Lamu is a perfect tropical retreat. Ideal for couples, honeymooners, or those seeking a relaxing escape on the Kenyan coast.

HIGHLIGHTS

Stay at a luxury beachfront resort
Explore historic Lamu Old Town
Optional dhow sunset cruise
Enjoy Swahili-inspired cuisine
White sand beaches and turquoise waters
Perfect for honeymoons and romantic getaways
Travel via SGR and domestic flight

ITINERARY

1

NAIROBI – MOMBASA – LAMU

Depart Nairobi aboard the morning SGR train to Mombasa. On arrival, connect to a domestic flight to Lamu via Moi International Airport. Transfer to The Majlis Resort or similar. Afternoon at leisure to relax or explore Shela beach. Dinner and overnight at the resort.

The Majlis Resort or similar

Dinner

2

FULL DAY LAMU

Enjoy a full day in Lamu. Optional activities include visiting Lamu Old Town (a UNESCO World Heritage Site), dhow sailing, snorkelling, or visiting Takwa ruins. Return to The Majlis Resort or similar for dinner and overnight stay.

The Majlis Resort or similar

Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

3

RELAXATION & BEACH LEISURE

Spend the day enjoying the beach, hotel amenities or book a massage/spa treatment. Optional sunset dhow cruise can be arranged. Overnight at The Majlis Resort or similar.

The Majlis Resort or similar

Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

4

LAMU – MOMBASA – NAIROBI

After breakfast, transfer to Manda Airport for your return flight to Mombasa. Upon arrival, board the SGR train back to Nairobi. Drop-off at Nairobi Terminus. End of trip.

Breakfast
